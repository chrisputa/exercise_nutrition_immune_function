## Aims and scope

This lecture series addresses the in-depth, systematic understanding of Exercise Nutrition and Immune Function in health and performance. It is focussed on interaction of Exercise (Patho)Physiology - Nutrition Science - Immune Function.


## Course Details

Here’s a summary of the key parameters of the Module **Natural sciences or social sciences** (optional module) (NSW) that is offered as part of the Bachelor Degree program Bachelor of Science Sportwissenschaft Performance and Health and Bachelor of Science in Nutrition Science at Friedrich-Schiller-University Jena.

The course has three components: 

1. Lectures
2. Interactive Dashboards
3. Tutorials

## Grading

Prerequisite for admission to the module examination:
- To achieve the learning objectives of this module, regular attendance at the module's seminars and exercises is required.
- Regular attendance is defined as missing no more than 15 percent of the class time in any given session.

Module examination (NSW) in the form of:
- Written exam, 60 min, pass/fail
